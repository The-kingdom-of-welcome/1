Our Program

The Formula

Real stories of transformation, hope, and belonging — from those who found their second home in Morocco.

The Kingdom of Welcome offers a comprehensive humanitarian program combining safe housing, personalized guidance, health support, and community integration. Below are the stories of those who've walked this path before you.

Marie
🇨🇩 Congo · Marrakech

Finding Peace in Marrakech

Marie fled the conflict in Congo with her young daughter. At The Kingdom of Welcome, she found a safe home in Marrakech's serene riads.

"This place is more than a refuge. It's Peace."

Aïcha now volunteers helping others adjust to life in Morocco, becoming a pillar of the community she once arrived at as a stranger.

Jamal
🇸🇾 Syria · Fez

A New Chapter in Morocco

Jamal arrived in Morocco seeking safety and opportunity. At our refuge, he received support to start a bakery business in Fez.

"Morocco gave me a chance to rebuild."

Jamal now employs other refugees and locals, creating connections through delicious pastries and a shared sense of belonging.

Lina
🇻🇪 Venezuela · Casablanca

From Struggle to Strength

Lina traveled far to escape hardship in Venezuela. At The Kingdom of Welcome, she accessed healthcare and language classes.

"This place helped me find my voice."

Lina now advocates for refugee rights, inspiring others to thrive in their new home and stand with dignity in a new land.
